Welcome to the SpaceRoyale Alpha Test!

Hello, and thank you for joining us tonight for the first ever SpaceRoyale.io alpha test.

If you are able to, please join our discord server by clicking https://discord.gg/me8Cy8E. You should be able to join via your web browser and listen in as a guest to the chat. If you can't get discord to work, no problem, just enjoy the game.

After you click play, you will be taken to a lobby. This lobby will hold all players until the game is ready to start. If we miss you for one game, you can join the next game. Waiting in the lobby is your best bet.

Please create an account by clicking Sign Up. Choose a color for your ship, make sure you have the name you want, and press Play!
